§3 · Color system

Warm ivory ground, one action color, one accent

A dominant pine-teal for every action, champagne-gold as editorial seasoning (decorative — never body text on light), warm-charcoal reserved for bands. Hex shown for reference; oklch is the shipped token.

Core palette
Safety Card ramp (1–4) — color is always paired with a number + label
Contrast (§3 note): Ink on Ivory/White clears AA (AAA for large). Pine-teal text on ivory passes AA. White on pine-teal passes AA for buttons. Gold is decorative only — for gold text, Antique Gold (--gold-deep) or gold on the dark band.

§4 · Typography

Playfair Display over Inter

High-contrast serif for display, neutral sans for everything else. The character comes from the contrast between the two and from generous sizing — not exotic faces. Body never drops below 14px.
